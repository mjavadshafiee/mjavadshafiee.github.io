Mohammad Javad Shafiee, is currently Research Assistant Professor in the Department of Systems Design Engineering at University of Waterloo. He received the B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence from Shiraz University, Shiraz, Iran, in 2008 and 2011 respectively; and the Ph.D. degree in systems design engineering from the University of Waterloo, Canada in 2017. His main research focus is on statistical learning and graphical models with random fields and deep learning approaches. His research interests include Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Biomedical Image Processing and his main focus is on Graphical Models specially Conditional Random Fields and Markov Random Fields.
